Moody's Analytics - Leaders in Credit Risk Management

Moody's Analytics helps risk practitioners across industries and regions respond to an evolving marketplace with confidence. Our award-winning data, models, analytical software, and expertise empower capital markets and risk professionals to better measure, monitor, and manager risk.

Moody's Analytics offers a large suite of Credit Risk products such as:

  • Moody's Credit View
  • Commercial Mortgage Metrics
  • CreditEdge
  • RiskCalc
  • RiskBench
  • Credit Research Database
